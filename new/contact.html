Frequently Asked Questions

  1. ARE YOU LICENSED AND INSURED?

    - Yes, Canyon Critters is insured, and we are also licensed by the USDA for our wolf shows. We always show all of our critters in a safe and responsible manner.

  2. HOW FAR WILL YOU TRAVEL?

    - Our critters travel around the entire greater metro Denver area, and Boulder and the Foothills. We have even performed as far north as Fort Collins and as far south as Colorado Springs. If you are interested in having Canyon Critters come to your event or party, give Nico a call at 303.642.0690.

  3. DO YOU HAVE AN AGE LIMIT?

    - No. Because we always show all of the critters in a safe and responsible manner, anyone who is interested and curious enough is welcome to attend a Canyon Critters reptile show or wolf show.

  4. I’M READY TO SCHEDULE A SHOW. HOW DO I CONTACT YOU?

    - You can call Nico at 303.642.0690, or you can contact him via email at nico@canyoncritters.com. You can also submit a question or a comment in the form to the right, or you can visit our contact page for more information.

  5. DO I NEED TO BE WORRIED ABOUT GERMS IF MY KIDS HANDLE THE REPTILES?

    - No. We emphasize proper hygiene by providing sanitizing wipes to disinfect your hands each time after touching the critters.

  6. CAN WE TAKE OUR OWN PHOTOS?

    - Yes, of course! There will be plenty of fun and unique photo opportunities for you to take advantage of, so be sure to have your camera ready.

If you have any additional questions or comments, you can submit them in the form above. Or you can call Nico at 303.642.0690, you can contact him via email at nico@canyoncritters.com, or you can visit our contact page for more information. We look forward to meeting all of your animal entertainment needs!
